# SEO, GEO, and AdSense readiness

The MVP will treat search visibility, generative search visibility, and future AdSense approval as product constraints from the start. Public locale routes and opportunity detail pages should be indexable, have localized metadata, hreflang alternatives, robots.txt, canonical URLs, Open Graph data, and JSON-LD structured data where the opportunity qualifies as an event. The MVP will not generate a full opportunity-detail sitemap from dynamic Base data; detail pages can be discovered through internal links, and a runtime sitemap can be added later if indexing evidence justifies it. Event JSON-LD is only used for records with enough event semantics, such as dates, location or online attendance, and an official entry URL; credits, beta access, and benefit-only opportunities are not forced into Event markup.

GEO means visibility in generative search experiences, not a separate markup system. The app will focus on useful, original, well-structured pages and will not add special LLM-only files or manipulative AI-search tactics.

AdSense code will not be integrated in the MVP. The app should only be prepared for future AdSense with bilingual About, Contact, Privacy, and Terms pages, stable navigation, and ad slot boundaries that do not obscure discovery or look like navigation.
